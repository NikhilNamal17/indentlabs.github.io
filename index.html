Indent Labs

Open source writing software

About

Indent Labs is, at its core, an ambitious open-source natural language processing project aimed squarely at moonshots in the field of writing. Wouldn't it be awesome if you could generate high-quality stories from outlines? Automatically outline your story? Generate a story as you make decisions on behalf of a character? What about pointing out logical inconsistencies you missed in writing, and suggesting solutions?

The first word processor showed up in the 60s and revolutionized writing through technology. Isn't it time for another shift forward?

Ankov, the Internet Beast [source]

Ankov is a modular entity that is free to roam the Internet, collectively learning and interacting with various communities around the web.

  • Huge source of natural language to process
  • Inherent feedback loops baked into societal integration
  • Excellent for testing natural language generation

overtheshoulder [source]

A collection of linguistic analytics aimed at performing authorship detection in real-time

  • Can potentially guess or correlate authors behind anonymous works
  • Applications in spam and plagiarism detection
  • Could improve personalized natural language generation with author fingerprint heuristics

Stegkov [source]

An automated steganographic library for encoding hidden messages in generated text

  • Currently supports Markov chain generation
  • Steganographic messages look like there's no hidden message at all
  • A secret key is required to unencode the hidden message
